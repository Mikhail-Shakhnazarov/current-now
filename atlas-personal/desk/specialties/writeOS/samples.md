# writeOS Sample Projects

Projects for demonstrating writeOS workflow.

---

## Research Summary -> Multi-Format

**Difficulty**: Beginner | **Sessions**: 1-2

Take a research abstract, transform to multiple audience-appropriate formats.

**Input**: 250-word abstract on any topic.
**Outputs**: Blog post (800 words), Twitter thread (8-10), Executive summary (1 page).

Exercises: Schema extraction from dense text, multiple transforms from single schema.

---

## Personal Essay -> Publication Suite

**Difficulty**: Beginner-Intermediate | **Sessions**: 2

Write personal essay, transform for different publication venues.

**Input**: 1000-word personal draft.
**Outputs**: Literary magazine, newsletter, LinkedIn post, podcast script.

Exercises: Extracting claims from personal writing, adapting tone while preserving meaning.

---

## Technical Explainer -> Multi-Level

**Difficulty**: Intermediate | **Sessions**: 2-3

Explain a technical concept, create versions for different expertise levels.

**Input**: Technical explanation you understand well.
**Outputs**: Expert version, intermediate version, beginner version, analogy-based version.

Exercises: Definition management, claim preservation across expertise levels.

---

## Argument Transformation

**Difficulty**: Intermediate | **Sessions**: 2

Take a position, transform for audiences with different priors.

**Input**: 500-word argument for a position.
**Outputs**: Sympathetic audience, skeptical audience, neutral audience, steelman of opposition.

Exercises: Maintaining claims while adapting rhetoric, evidence selection.

---

## Grant Proposal Pipeline

**Difficulty**: Advanced | **Sessions**: 3-4

Develop project idea through to complete grant proposal.

**Input**: Rough notes on a project.
**Outputs**: Concept note, full proposal, budget justification, support letter template, slide deck.

Exercises: Complex schema with many claim types, formal constraints, cross-document consistency.
